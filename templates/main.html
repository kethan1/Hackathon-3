Welcome to Space Invaders

How to Play

  • Use Right and Left Arrow Keys to Move Right and Left
  • Use Space to Shoot

Point System:

  • You win where you reach 50 points
  • You loose when you reach 0 points
  • When an asteroid crosses the border 15 points are lost
  • When an alien crosses the border 5 points are lost
  • When an alien bullet hits you 2 point is lost
  • When you kill an alien 5 points are gained. Remeber that to kill an alien you need to shoot it twice.
  • When you shoot a meteor 2 points are gained
